OUR STORY




hGRILL, millennial’s much-loved American eatery, first opened doors in the beautiful city of New York in 2016. Started by techie-turned-chef, XYZ,hGRILL made humble beginnings from a small space in New York and have expanded to multiple locations across North India. Founder, XYZ is focussed on creating recipes right from the heart of America, from sliders, sausages, waffles, to much more. All the recipes have the originality of the States intact. hGRILL'S remains focussed on bringing the best of American food, with a quality that surpasses everything else you have ever tried.Be it the hot dogs straight from the streets of New York. The dining experience at hGRILL's is not just palate-pleasing, but is also a visual treat to the eyes. The brand is well known for its quirky food presentations, especially with the mouth-watering fries served in beakers, famous injectors, and more. Rightly so, hGRILL's is a regular recipient of the Times Food & Nightlife Awards for 4 years in a row, with many more accolades to its credit.
